Modern Orthodox Saints
Photo Date Description
July 12th St. Paisios the Athonite was a 20th-century Orthodox Christian monk known for his deep humility, wisdom, and spiritual counsel. He lived on Mount Athos and became widely revered for his miracles and guidance. He was canonized in 2015. His Feast day falls on July 12th
August 16th St. Joseph the Hesychast was a 20th-century Athonite monk and spiritual father known for his life of intense prayer, silence (hesychia), and asceticism. He revived the practice of the Jesus Prayer on Mount Athos and inspired many monastic communities. He was canonized in 2020. His Feast day falls on August 16th
November 15th St. Herman of Alaska was an 18th-century Russian Orthodox monk and missionary who brought the Christian faith to the native peoples of Alaska. He is known for his humility, compassion, and defense of Indigenous rights. He is considered the first Orthodox saint of North America.
